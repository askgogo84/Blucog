What AI-generated documents look like to FraudLens
AI-generated statements lack original source files or trails. FraudLens detects: synthetic formatting inconsistencies, font fingerprints from AI tools (ChatGPT, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E), metadata showing no originating bank software, pixel-level distortions from image manipulation, and template patterns not matching any genuine bank format in its 10M+ document database.
Why traditional OCR tools fail
OCR reads what's on the page — it doesn't question whether the page itself is authentic. A perfectly formatted AI-generated bank statement with plausible transaction history will pass any OCR check. FraudLens examines the document's forensic DNA: its file history, metadata, pixel layers, and structural patterns — not just its content.
